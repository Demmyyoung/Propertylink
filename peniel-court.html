Peniel Coast Gardens

Akodo Ise, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos

Description

Peniel Coast Gardens (frequently referred to as Peniel Court) is a prestigious mixed-use land development designed for residential and commercial investment. Located in the rapidly evolving Akodo Ise area of Ibeju-Lekki, it is situated perfectly for those seeking to capitalize on the region's massive industrial growth.

The estate is in close proximity to major projects like the Dangote Refinery and the Lekki Free Trade Zone, making it a highly desirable asset with high appreciation potential. Its strategic location offers a blend of tranquility and industrial progress.
